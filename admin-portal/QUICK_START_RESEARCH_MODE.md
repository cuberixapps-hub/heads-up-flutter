- **Research Mode (RECOMMENDED)**: Premium quality with full reasoning
- **Standard Mode**: Legacy basic generation (not recommended)

### Target Audience
- **Universal**: Works for everyone
- **Teens**: Social media, gaming, viral trends
- **Adults**: Nostalgia + mainstream entertainment
- **Families**: Kid-friendly, multi-generational

### Delay Between Generations
- **Recommended**: 10-30 seconds
- Gives AI time to do proper research
- Avoids rate limiting

### Countries Per Deck
- **Recommended**: 3 countries
- Each deck works in UNIVERSAL + your selected countries
- Balanced distribution across regions

- **Solution**: System is working! It validates topics and only accepts 7+ scores

**Problem**: Taking too long
- **Solution**: Research takes time! Be patient for quality results
