## 🔬 Research Mode vs Standard Mode

### **Research Mode (RECOMMENDED)** 🌟

Generates decks with comprehensive research and reasoning:

- **Trending Analysis**: Why the topic is HOT right now (with specific data/stats)
- **Cultural Relevance**: Why it resonates with the target country/region
- **Audience Appeal**: Why the specific demographic will love it
- **Supporting Data**: Real evidence from box office, streaming stats, social media, etc.
- **Quality Scores**:
  - 🔥 Viral Potential (1-10)
  - 👁️ Recognition Score (1-10)
  - 🎮 Playability Score (1-10)
- **Why It Works**: Comprehensive explanation of the deck's appeal

### **Standard Mode** (Legacy)

Basic AI generation without detailed research (old system).

## 📊 Quality Metrics Explained

Each researched deck is scored across three dimensions:

### 🔥 **Viral Potential (1-10)**

How likely is this deck to be shared and talked about?

- **7-8**: Strong viral potential, timely topic
- **9-10**: Extremely viral, trending phenomenon

### 👁️ **Recognition Score (1-10)**

How well-known are the items in this deck to the target audience?

- **7-8**: Most people will recognize these
- **9-10**: Household names, universally known

### 🎮 **Playability Score (1-10)**

How fun is this deck to actually play in Heads Up!?

- **7-8**: Good variety, fun to act out
- **9-10**: Perfect game mechanics, highly engaging

## 📝 How Research Mode Works

### Step 1: Country Selection

System automatically selects countries with lowest deck count for balanced distribution.

### Step 2: Deep Trending Analysis

AI analyzes:

- Current box office hits
- Streaming platform trends
- Social media viral content
- Sports events and cultural moments
- Regional preferences and passions

### Step 3: Topic Generation with Evidence

Generates topics with:

- Specific trending reason (backed by data)
- Cultural relevance explanation
- Target audience appeal analysis
- Supporting data sources (box office, streaming stats, viral metrics)
- Example cards to demonstrate playability

### Step 4: Quality Validation

Each topic is scored:

- Viral Potential: Will people share this?
- Recognition: Will audience know these items?
- Playability: Is it fun to act out?

Only topics scoring 7+ across all metrics are accepted.

### Step 5: Multi-Difficulty Card Generation

Generates 3 difficulty modes in ONE deck:

- 🟢 **Easy**: 15 simple, recognizable cards
- 🟡 **Medium**: 20 moderate challenge cards
- 🔴 **Hard**: 25 expert-level cards

### Step 6: Enhanced Description

Deck includes:

- Standard description
- Full trending reason
- Complete "Why it works" explanation
- All research metadata saved to database

## 🚀 How to Use Research Mode

### In the Automated Deck Generator:

1. **Enable Research Mode** (recommended)

   - Toggle "Research Mode" in the control panel
   - This is the default setting

2. **Select Target Audience** (optional)

   - Choose: Universal, Teens, Adults, or Families
   - Leave as Universal for broad appeal

3. **Configure Settings**

   - Set delay between generations (recommended: 10-30 seconds)
   - Set countries per deck (recommended: 3)

4. **Start Automation**

   - Click "Start Automation"
   - Watch the detailed research output in the activity log

5. **Review Generated Decks**
   - See full research breakdown
   - Quality scores displayed
   - All reasoning visible
