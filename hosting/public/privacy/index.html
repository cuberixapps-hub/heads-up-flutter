2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the game experience
  • Track game scores and leaderboard rankings
  • Manage subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Send push notifications (with your permission)
  • Diagnose crashes and technical issues
  • Display relevant advertisements (free tier)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting — Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: Deck content storage — Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management — Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Advertisements (free tier only) — Privacy Policy

4. Advertising

The free version of the App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use device advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. Premium subscribers do not see advertisements. You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

5. Camera & Microphone

The App may request access to your camera and microphone to record gameplay reaction videos. This is entirely optional. Recorded videos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

6. Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications for daily deck reminders, streak updates, and feature announcements. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or the App's settings screen.

7. Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as your account is active. Anonymous accounts and their data may be automatically deleted after 12 months of inactivity. You can request data deletion at any time (see Section 10).

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure cloud infrastructure (Firebase, Supabase), and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and data (via Settings > Clear Data, or by contacting us)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Disable push notifications, camera, and microphone access
  • Export your data upon request

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the App or by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
